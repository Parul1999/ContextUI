Badge

The Badge generates a small badge to the top-right of its child(ren).
Example - To show a person is online/offline

Examples

The Badge aplied on three types of Avatar :

  1. Letter
  2. Image
  3. Icon

You can choose any icon/image/letter of your choice and add a badge to it

• Badge on Letter Avatar

Badge on letter avatar , can be used to represent the status(online/offline) of the person. The status can be changed to offline or online.

  1. Online : badge-online
  2. Offline : badge-offline

   <div class="letter-badge" title="Parul Kohli">
      <span class="letter-badge-text">PK</span>

   </div>
