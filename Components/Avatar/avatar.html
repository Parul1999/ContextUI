Avatar

The Avatar component is used to represent users. There are various ways to represent the same.

Examples

There are three types of Avatar :

  1. Letter
  2. Image
  3. Icon

You can choose any icon/image/letter of your choice and make it an avatar

• Letter Avatar

Letter Avatar is a type of Avatar in which the initials could be seen and on hovering we could see the whole name.
Example - As seen on contacts
